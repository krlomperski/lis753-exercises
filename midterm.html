Friends of Palo Alto Library - Used Book Sales

Find great used books and support the library.

About the Used Book Sale

Every month, the Friends of Palo Alto Library offer more than 50,000 used books, records, videos, magazines, and more. Most items come from donations by individuals, estates, and companies, as well as books removed from the Palo Alto Library's shelves to make room for new titles.

Main Room

The Main Room is along the north driveway next to the Charleston Shopping Center. Here you will find a wide selection of general books at low prices.

  • Hardcover books start at $1
  • Paperback books start at 50¢
  • You can also drop off large book donations in this room

Children's Room

Children's books and family items are in a separate room near the Cubberley track, Pavilion and Greendell School. Many items cost 50¢ or $1.

  • Picture books
  • Fiction and nonfiction for school-age readers
  • Non-English language books
  • CDs and DVDs
  • Books for parents and teachers

Saturday only: During the first hour of the sale, please choose no more than 12 books at a time and bring them to the cashiers to hold.

Bargain Rooms

Bargain books are in rooms K6 and K7. Prices are even lower here:

  • Hardcover books: $1
  • Paperbacks: 50¢
  • Children's books: 25¢

On Sunday, prices in the Bargain Room are half-off. You can also buy a grocery bag for $5 and fill it with books.

The Children's and Bargain Rooms open one hour earlier than the Main Room on Saturday.

Gift Certificates

Gift certificates are available at the sale in $5 amounts. They make an easy gift for any book lover.

Tickets and Entry on Saturday

Some customers come early to get a good place in line. To keep things fair, we use a simple ticket system at the Main Room on Saturday:

  • Tickets are handed out starting at 8:00 a.m.
  • Each person may receive up to two tickets
  • Your ticket number holds your place in line for the Main Room

You can visit the Children's and Bargain Rooms or get coffee after you receive your ticket. Please return a few minutes before the Main Room opens. If you miss your time, you will lose your place in line and join the end of the line.

If tickets run out, you can still join the later part of the line.

Capacity and Book Limits

Because of space and safety rules, we can allow only 160 customers in the Main Room at one time.

  • Customers are admitted in ticket order from 8:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.
  • Each person may take only 12 books off the shelves at a time
  • After you buy those books and exit, you may rejoin the line at the north door
  • The 12-book rule may continue past noon if there is still a line outside

Why Shop This Sale?

The sale offers tens of thousands of books at low prices. All proceeds go directly to support the Palo Alto Library.

  • 100% of your purchase supports the library
  • No sales tax is charged
  • Friendly volunteers are available to help you
